# Release 20260701 - July 1st 2026

## API

- The MeteoSwiss climate normals data are now available for download in the data collection _ch.meteoschweiz.ogd-climate-normals-grid_ also in COG TIFF format. With this step, the previous data collections will soon be taken out of service. For details, see [End-of-life](https://docs.geo.admin.ch/page/end-of-life.html)
- The MeteoSwiss hail hazard data are now available for download in the data collection _ch.meteoschweiz.ogd-radar-derived-grid_ also in COG TIFF format. With this step, the previous data collections will soon be taken out of service. For details, see [End-of-life](https://docs.geo.admin.ch/page/end-of-life.html)
- The _ch.bazl.luftfahrthindernis_ layer group contains a significantly increased number of objects from now on. Poles and individual segments of high-voltage transmission lines are now available in the dataset.
- The layer _ch.swisstopo.geologie-geocover_ will be replaced by 6 new layers in course of an upcoming new data product swissGEOCOVER2D later in 2026. Details to follow soon.
- 14 layers from swisstopo's Swiss Geological Survey will be decommissioned later in 2026. For details, see [End-of-life](https://docs.geo.admin.ch/page/end-of-life.html)
- For some layers, the `LegendURL` returned in the WMS GetCapabilities response now points to the official legend image hosted on api3.geo.admin.ch instead of the WMS GetLegendGraphic URL. The GetLegendGraphic request itself remains unchanged.
- [Full changelog](https://github.com/geoadmin/mf-chsdi3/compare/2026-05-06-rc1...2026-07-01-rc1)
