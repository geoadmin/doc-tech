# Release 20260701 - July 1st 2026

## API

- The MeteoSwiss climate normals data are now available for download in the data collection *ch.meteoschweiz.ogd-climate-normals-grid* also in COG TIFF format. With this step, the previous data collections will soon be taken out of service. For details, see [End-of-life](https://docs.geo.admin.ch/page/end-of-life.html)
- The MeteoSwiss hail hazard data are now available for download in the data collection *ch.meteoschweiz.ogd-radar-derived-grid* also in COG TIFF format. With this step, the previous data collections will soon be taken out of service. For details, see [End-of-life](https://docs.geo.admin.ch/page/end-of-life.html)
- The layer *ch.swisstopo.geologie-geocover* will be replaced by 6 new layers in course of an upcoming new data product swissGEOCOVER2D later in 2026. Details to follow soon.
- 14 layers from swisstopo's Swiss Geological Survey will be decommissioned later in 2026. For details, see [End-of-life](https://docs.geo.admin.ch/page/end-of-life.html)
- [Full changelog](https://github.com/geoadmin/mf-chsdi3/compare/2026-05-06-rc1...2026-07-01-rc1)
